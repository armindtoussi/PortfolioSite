About Me

Welcome to my site, I am Armin Toussi, and I am currently a software development student at BCIT in Vancouver, BC Canada. I am a passionite problem solver that loves to work, and has natural leadership skills. At currently 30 years old, this is my second go around in the career department. I was in the culinary industry for over 10 years, but I fell out of love with cooking and as it is an industry of passion, I was unable to continue. Because of my previous experience in the culinary industry, I have developed great people skills, time management skills, and most of all the understanding of the bigger picture. I was responsible for day to day runnings of the kitchen as well as being part of short term and long term planning, and I think this gives me an advantage over generic graduate students. I understand what it takes to succeed.
