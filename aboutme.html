Introduction

Hi there, my name is Armin Toussi, I'm 30 years old and i'm currently a software development student. I have experience working with object-oriented programming, specfically the Java language. I also have knowledge in HTML, CSS, and some Javascript, PHP, and Ajax. My focus in school is on programming applications and not so much on web. To that end I have created this section on my site for you to get to know me a little.

That's me there above showing off my fun side at my mothers wedding in 2012. It may seem like i'm all fun and games, far from it actually. I am an extremely hard-working person with a strong work ethic. As I said, i'm currently a student so my technical skills aren't fully developed however, i'm an extremely fast learner. I like to problem solve which is one of the things that led me into this field, but I am also just interested in how computing and applications work. As a self-starter I am capable of taking on a project and figuring things out that I don't understand with minimal questions. I am eager to learn as much as I can and impact any project that I work on.

My Background

Well for 10 years I was a chef and fell out of love with cooking so I decided to take a u-turn and go to school for another passion of mine which was computing. So here I am, finished term One of the Computer Systems Technology Program at BCIT. There are a lot of skills that I took away from being a chef for so long. I learned how to cope and produce under a large amount of pressure, I learned how to work in a team but also alone, I learned how to communicate concisely and effectively, and possibly most importantly I learned how to not take ineffective shorcuts and to just do things right. I think these are very important transferable skills that could be of use to any employer.

Present
